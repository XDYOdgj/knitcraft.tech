Flintar Replacement Filter for HomeLabs H13 Air Purifier

Upgrade your air purification experience with the Flintar Replacement Filter, designed specifically for the HomeLabs H13 air purifier. This 3-in-1 filter set combines a pre-filter, activated carbon filter, and an H13 grade main filter to deliver superior indoor air quality.

With advanced filtration technology, the Flintar Filter captures up to 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. That means it effectively removes dust, pet hair, allergens, and other harmful particles from your home or office environment, helping you breathe easier and live healthier.

The activated carbon component is perfect for eliminating unpleasant odors from everyday life, such as cooking, smoking, or pet-related smells. It also absorbs volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that can impact indoor air quality and cause health issues.

This filter is easy to install and requires no special tools. Simply replace it every 3 months or after 2100 hours of use for optimal performance and consistent fresh air. The 2-pack design ensures you always have a spare filter on hand, avoiding frequent replacements and keeping your space clean and comfortable for longer.

Whether you're caring for pets, managing indoor pollutants, or just looking to improve your living environment, the Flintar Replacement Filter is a reliable and efficient choice. It's built to last and offers excellent value for your air purifier.
