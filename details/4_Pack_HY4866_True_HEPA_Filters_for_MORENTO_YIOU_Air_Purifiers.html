WILERDA HY4866 Replacement Filters

Experience superior air quality with our advanced 4-pack replacement filters designed for maximum efficiency and lasting performance.

Superior Air Purification

Our WILERDA HY4866 replacement filters utilize H13 True HEPA technology to capture microscopic pollutants and allergens that can impact your health. This high-efficiency filter removes up to 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.03 microns, ensuring cleaner air for you and your family.

Three-Stage Filtration System

These filters feature a three-stage design, beginning with a pre-filter that traps larger dust and debris, followed by the HEPA filter that removes fine particles, and ending with a honeycomb activated carbon filter that absorbs odors and reduces volatile organic compounds (VOCs) for a fresher, healthier indoor environment.

Designed for Specific Models

Engineered for use with MORENTO HY4866, HY4866-WF, and YIOU M1 air purifiers, these filters are built for compatibility and performance. The enhanced version includes electrostatic cotton, which increases filtration efficiency and extends the lifespan of the filters.

Eco-Friendly and Cost-Effective

The 4-pack includes all necessary pre-filters, giving you convenience and peace of mind without compromising on quality or performance.
