isinlive Replacement Filters for AP303/AP304 Air Purifiers

Designed to offer top-grade air filtration, these isinlive replacement filters are a perfect match for your DAYETTE or FRESHDEW AP303/AP304 air purifier. With a 3-stage filtration system, they ensure that your indoor air is clean, fresh, and safe from harmful pollutants. The pre-filter captures larger particles like dust and hair, the HEPA filter traps microscopic contaminants such as pollen and bacteria, and the activated carbon filter effectively neutralizes smoke, odors, and other airborne toxins.

Each filter is made from FSC-Certified paper, making them not only eco-friendly but also safe for your family's well-being. The 4-pack provides an ideal solution for regular use and maintenance, ensuring you always have replacement filters on hand. It's easy to install, and the on-device indicator light makes it simple to know when it's time to replace them again.
